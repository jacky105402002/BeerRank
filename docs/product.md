# BeerRank Product

## Positioning
BeerRank is a social beer rating web app. It lets beer lovers post beer photos, write short tasting notes, assign structured scores, like posts, and discover ranked beers.

## Core Users
- Casual beer drinker: wants to remember what they drank and whether it was worth buying again.
- Craft beer enthusiast: wants structured rating, comparison, and ranking.
- Discovery user: wants to browse popular beers before buying or ordering.

## MVP Value
The MVP combines three behaviors in one loop:
1. Capture: post a beer photo with a rating and note.
2. React: other users browse and like posts.
3. Rank: ratings and likes roll up into beer leaderboards.

## MVP Boundaries
In scope:
- Third-party login.
- User profile basics.
- Beer rating post with one photo.
- Structured rating and short text review.
- Feed sorted by recent activity.
- Like/unlike on posts.
- Beer leaderboard.

Out of scope:
- Native mobile app.
- Direct messages.
- Friend graph and advanced social graph.
- Merchant back office.
- Payments.
- Advanced recommendation algorithm.
- Multi-photo albums.
- Full moderation console.

## Product Principles
- Posting should feel as quick as publishing a social photo.
- Ratings must be structured enough to build rankings.
- Ranking rules must be understandable to users.
- Beer data should be reusable across many posts.

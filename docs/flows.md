# Flows

Status: Draft.

Primary source:
- `docs/specs/feature-beer-rating-mvp-flow-architecture.md`

Initial flows:
- Third-party login before protected actions.
- Create beer rating post with one photo.
- AI-assisted beer matching and user confirmation.
- Browse feed and like/unlike a post.
- View beer leaderboard.
- Open Beer Detail to inspect ranking contributors and reviews.

Alignment rule:
- A post contributes to rankings only after it has a confirmed Beer association, photo, rating, and published status.
